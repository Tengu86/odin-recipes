Orzo Salad

Description

This delicious Orzo salad is a must have for summer meals. A light and vibrant addition to and BBQ.

Ingredients

  • 1 box orzo pasta(cooked)
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 a red onion, petite diced
  • 1 cup feta cheese
  • one cup greek dressing
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine Pasta, Tomato, Cucumber and Red Onion in a large mixing bowl and stir until evenly mixed.
  2. Stir in Greek Dressing
  3. Add crumbled Feta and stir
  4. Add S&P to taste
  5. Serve immediatley or chill. Keeps refrigerated for one week.
